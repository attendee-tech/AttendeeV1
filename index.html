Streamline Student Attendance Tracking

Attendee helps educational institutions efficiently manage student attendance, improve accountability, and enhance academic performance.

Powerful Features

Attendee offers a comprehensive set of tools designed to simplify attendance management and improve student engagement.

Real-time Attendance Tracking

Record and monitor student attendance in real-time with our intuitive digital check-in system. No more paper registers or manual data entry.

Automated Notifications

Send automatic alerts to students and Lecturer about absences, ensuring timely communication and reducing administrative workload.

Comprehensive Reports

Generate detailed attendance reports by student, class, or date range. Identify patterns and take proactive measures to improve attendance rates.

Student Profiles

Access comprehensive student profiles with attendance history, academic performance, and contact information all in one place.

Customizable Settings

Tailor the system to your institution's specific needs with flexible attendance policies, grading scales, and notification preferences.

Secure Data Management

Keep student information safe with our robust security measures, role-based access controls, and regular data backups.
